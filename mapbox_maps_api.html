"Where's The Food?"

Citizen Fox

Citizen fox is an irish bar with delicious food choices like Hot Wings, Onion Rings, Burgers, Salads and fried chicken. Everyone is bound to find something to rave about. Not only is the food selection great but if you take a gander at their drink selection this would be the ultimate pre-game dinner spot.

Le Petit Fric

Located in the heart of downtown Mons, Le Petit Fric is an authentic Vietnamese restaurant. They have large Pho dishes, amazing spring rolls and so many main dish choices. My personal favorite dish is the Marmite Tofu which is grilled tofu with vegatables and steamed rice topped with a sweet homemade sauce.

La Primavera

If you love classic italian foods you La Primavera will not disappoint. Their pizzas are made fresh in a brick oven and there are so many varieties to choose from. They also have classic pastas like spaghetti bolognese, alfredo pasta and even the belgian staple Mussels!
